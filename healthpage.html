Wakinga Community Health Centre

Building hope, saving lives - One health facility at a time on Mfangano Island

10,000 People Served
30km To Nearest Facility

The Critical Situation

Life-Threatening Distance

The Wakinga community of 10,000 residents must travel 30km on miserable gravel roads to reach the nearest health facility.

Maternal Deaths

Many delivering mothers and infants die while being carried by hand to distant health facilities, with no vehicles available on the island.

Overcrowded Facilities

Sick people line up for 5-6 hours in direct sunlight at the few existing facilities, leading to preventable deaths.

Our Solution: Wakinga Health Centre

We're building a fully functional health centre to serve the Wakinga community, reducing the deadly journey to distant facilities and providing immediate, life-saving care.

Our development division focuses on improving the delivery of high-impact health products and services to the community's poorest households, helping expand access to health coverage where it's needed most.
