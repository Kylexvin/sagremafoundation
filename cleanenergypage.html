THE CHALLENGE

üèùÔ∏è Mfangano Island, Lake Victoria

Mfangano Island, located in Lake Victoria, is a remote and isolated region far from Kenya's major towns and cities. Due to its geographical location and limited infrastructure, access to reliable electricity remains a serious challenge for many families.

In communities such as Wakinga, most homes live in complete darkness once the sun sets. This creates numerous challenges that affect every aspect of daily life, from education and healthcare to economic opportunities and basic safety.

COMMUNITY-DRIVEN APPROACH

ü§ù Long-Term Impact Strategy

This project is community-driven and designed to create long-term impact. We work closely with local leaders, train community members in solar system maintenance, and ensure sustainable operation of all installations.

Our approach emphasizes local ownership, capacity building, and environmental stewardship to create lasting change that benefits future generations.

We believe that sustainable development comes from within the community. By involving local residents in every step of the process - from planning and installation to maintenance and expansion - we ensure that this project will continue to benefit Wakinga for years to come.

Training programs will equip community members with the skills needed to maintain and troubleshoot solar systems, creating local employment opportunities and ensuring project sustainability.
