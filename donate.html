Get Involved

Support Mfangano Island Community Development

We work with partner organizations to help solve Mfangano Island's unique and challenging problems. Our Foundation's programs help connect government agencies, donors, well-wishers, NGOs and other community organizations with the schools, health care units, fishermen, local community groups and households/families members on Mfangano and its sister islands.

Our partnerships create opportunities for volunteering, fund-raising, and organic farming field demonstrations.
