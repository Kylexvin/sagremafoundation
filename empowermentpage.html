🌟 Empowering the Marginalized

Building self-reliance and reducing poverty

ABOUT

Empowering the marginalized builds self-reliance and reduces poverty.

Our comprehensive approach focuses on providing sustainable livelihood opportunities and financial literacy to those who need it most. Through targeted interventions and community-based programs, we work to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting change.

We focus on:

👥

Widows and Widowers

Supporting those who have lost their spouses with sustainable income opportunities and community support.

🏠

Less Fortunate Families

Providing pathways out of poverty through skills training and economic empowerment programs.

🎓

Qualified Youth

Helping educated youth who struggle to find employment by creating entrepreneurship opportunities.

Empowerment Activities Include:

🌱

Community Organic Farming

Food security and income generation through sustainable agricultural practices

🏦

Table Banking & Merry-go-rounds

Accessible loans for small businesses through community-based financial systems

🐔

Poultry Farming

Low-cost, high-impact entrepreneurship opportunities for sustainable income

🐟

Fishing Support

Sustainable income from the lake through improved fishing techniques and equipment

🏛️ Community-Led Loan Program

The loan program is run entirely by local village women. As an end-goal, it seeks to transition experienced borrowers into independent banking in their community's formal economy.

Join us to give women and youths the opportunity to lift themselves and their families out of poverty!
