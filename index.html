Empowering Mfangano Island Community

Creating sustainable development through healthcare, education, clean water, and environmental conservation for 37,000 people on Mfangano Island, Lake Victoria, Kenya.

Explore Our Projects Support Our Mission

Our Purpose

Guided by our vision and driven by our mission to transform lives on Mfangano Island

Our Vision

To create a sustainable future for Mfangano and its siater islands in Lake Victoria, Kenya.

Our Mission

To empower the people of Mfangano Island through community-driven development initiated programs focusing on healthcare access, educational opportunities, clean water provision, women and youth empowerment, and environmental conservation.

Our Projects

Transforming lives through targeted community development initiatives

Community Health Centre

Providing essential healthcare services including maternal health, immunizations, and treatment for common diseases. Our mobile clinic reaches remote villages monthly.

15,000+ patients served
Learn More

Clean Drinking Water

Installing boreholes, water tanks, and purification systems to ensure access to safe drinking water for all island residents.

8 boreholes installed
Learn More

Clean Energy & Solar Power

Installing solar panels and renewable energy systems to provide reliable electricity for homes, schools, and health facilities across the island communities.

50+ solar installations
Learn More

Food Security & Agriculture

Training farmers in modern techniques, providing seeds and tools, and establishing community gardens to ensure food security year-round.

500+ farmers trained
Learn More

Women & Youth Empowerment

Skills training, microfinance programs, and leadership development to empower women and youth to become community leaders and entrepreneurs.

300+ youth trained
Learn More

Education Support

Scholarship programs, school infrastructure improvements, and adult literacy classes to ensure education access for all age groups.

200+ scholarships awarded
Learn More

Environmental Conservation

Tree planting initiatives, waste management programs, and sustainable fishing practices to preserve Lake Victoria's ecosystem.

10,000+ trees planted
