VOLUNTEER WITH US

Make a real difference in the lives of 37,000 people on Mfangano Island and surrounding areas. Whether you're a student, professional, or someone passionate about community development, we have meaningful volunteer opportunities that match your skills and interests.

Volunteer Opportunities

🎓 Education Support

Teach in local schools, provide tutoring, develop educational materials, and support teacher training programs.

🏥 Healthcare Assistance

Support community health initiatives, health education campaigns, and assist local healthcare workers.

🌱 Agricultural Projects

Work on organic farming initiatives, permaculture projects, and sustainable agriculture training.

💡 Skills Training

Share your professional skills in business, technology, marketing, or other specialized areas.

💰 Microfinance Support

Help with financial literacy programs, loan management, and economic empowerment initiatives.

🌍 Environmental Conservation

Participate in environmental protection projects and community awareness campaigns.

STAY & VISIT

Experience authentic community life on beautiful Mfangano Island! We welcome visitors from around the globe to stay with us, whether you're volunteering, exploring as a tourist, or simply passing through. Live with our family, become part of the community, and create unforgettable memories.

🌟 Full Board Accommodation

Stay in our family home with comfortable accommodation and three delicious meals daily

Only Kshs 1,000 per day

Includes: Accommodation + Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner

What You'll Experience

🏝️Island Life

Wake up to stunning Lake Victoria views, experience traditional Suba culture, and enjoy the peaceful island atmosphere.

🤝Community Integration

Participate in daily community activities, local markets, cultural events, and traditional ceremonies.

🍽️Local Cuisine

Enjoy authentic local meals prepared with fresh ingredients from our organic gardens and Lake Victoria.

📚Cultural Learning

Learn about local traditions, participate in community projects, and gain insights into rural Kenyan life.
